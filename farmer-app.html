Farmer Features

Core user needs

Daily mortality entry

Enter mortality shed-wise, culls, cause notes, and photo proof when needed.

Feed stock by shed

Track how many bags are in each shed and total balance available on the farm.

Feed inward register

Log on which day feed came, how many bags arrived, and whether it was pre-starter, starter, grower, or finisher.

Medicine inventory and usage

See available medicines, required medicines, and when each medicine was given to birds.

Operational value

Farm-side data entry

The app is no longer only for viewing. It also captures mortality, feed bags, medicines, and farm activities.

Farm Entries

What the farmer can put into the app

Bird Data

Daily flock updates

  • Mortality by shed
  • Cull birds and reason
  • Current bird balance
  • Body-weight sample entry
  • Water intake or unusual drop alerts

Feed Data

Inventory and inward

  • Bags available in each shed
  • Total bags left on farm
  • Feed received date-wise
  • Feed type: pre-starter, starter, grower, finisher
  • Damaged or wet bag reporting

Medicine Data

Stock and dosing

  • Medicines currently in stock
  • Medicines required urgently
  • Date/time medicine was given
  • Medicine used for which shed or batch
  • Pending medicine request to company

More Useful Farm Options

Additional things the farmer app should capture

Operations

Day-to-day shed controls

  • Temperature and humidity entry
  • Water tank level and water issue reporting
  • Litter condition notes
  • Power cut / DG run hours
  • Bird picking or abnormal behavior report

Coordination

Communication with company

  • Raise feed shortage request
  • Raise medicine requirement request
  • Acknowledge officer visit remarks
  • Ask for catch/lift planning updates
  • Upload photos of issues in shed

Records

Farmer-side traceability

  • Batch-wise document vault
  • Past cycle comparison
  • Payment advice and statement download
  • Medicine and feed consumption history
  • Notification center for due actions
