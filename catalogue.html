Client Problems

What this software solves

Problem 1

Manual registers

Mortality, feed, and medicine records are scattered or delayed.

Problem 2

Low visibility

Management does not get real-time status from farms and field teams.

Problem 3

Payment disputes

Farmers often lack transparent settlement and usage history.

Our Solution

How the platform addresses it

Digitization

One system

All key farm, field, and finance records live in a common workflow.

Traceability

Role-based data capture

Farmers, field officers, and admins each update what they own.

Control

Alerts and approvals

Issues become visible early and important decisions stay auditable.

Module 1

Farmer and Shed Management

  • Farmer onboarding and profile
  • KYC, contract, and bank details
  • Farm geo-tagging and shed capacity
  • Readiness and compliance score
  • Field officer mapping

Module 2

Batch Management

  • Batch creation and placement planning
  • Chick quantity and source tracking
  • Bird-age timeline and growth tracking
  • Lift readiness and batch closure
  • Batch history and comparisons

Module 3

Daily Flock Monitoring

  • Daily mortality and cull entries
  • Body-weight samples
  • Bird balance by batch and shed
  • Health observations and abnormality notes
  • Trend and alert tracking

Module 4

Feed Management

  • Feed inward entries date-wise
  • Feed type tracking: pre-starter, starter, grower, finisher
  • Bags received and allocated by shed
  • Feed stock left in each shed
  • Total bags available on farm

Module 5

Medicine and Health Register

  • Available medicines on farm
  • Required medicines and shortage alerts
  • Medicine usage log by date
  • Medicine given to which shed or flock
  • Vaccination and treatment reminders

Module 6

Field Operations

  • Field officer visit scheduling
  • Visit checklist and inspection records
  • Photo uploads and issue escalation
  • Farmer remarks and compliance notes
  • Revisit and action tracking

Module 7

Lift and Settlement

  • Lift planning and final count
  • Final live weight recording
  • Growing charge and incentive calculation
  • Deductions and exception review
  • Payment status and farmer ledger

Module 8

Reports and Analytics

  • Zone-wise batch performance
  • Mortality and FCR analytics
  • Feed and medicine usage reports
  • Farmer ranking and compliance analysis
  • Settlement and profitability reports

Roles

Who will use the software

  • Admin and management users
  • Operations and finance teams
  • Field officers and supervisors
  • Farmers with mobile access
  • Optional sales or live-bird dispatch users

Farmer App Features

What the farmer can do in the software

Daily entries

Mortality and culls

Farmer can update mortality shed-wise with remarks and photos.

Feed controls

Shed-wise stock

Farmer can enter bags received, bags consumed, and current balance by shed.

Medicine controls

Usage and requirement

Farmer can record available medicines, required medicines, and when they were given to birds.

Support and trust

Ledger and reminders

Farmer can see payments, upcoming actions, and officer remarks without repeated calls.

Field App Features

What the field officer can do

Visits

Planned route and priority alerts

Officers see farm routes, pending tasks, and urgency flags.

Inspection

Checklist-based reviews

Capture litter, feed, water, health, temperature, and compliance observations.

Escalation

Photos and issue reports

Raise incidents for mortality, disease, medicine need, or infrastructure issues.

Verification

Field validation

Cross-check farmer entries with visit data and update corrective actions.
