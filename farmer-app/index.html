Core Workflows

What the farmer can do inside the app.

The app is built around actual farm-side records instead of generic mobile features, helping growers submit clearer daily operational information.

Mortality entry

Daily mortality and culls recorded batch-wise and shed-wise.

Feed bags by shed

Track how many bags are available in each shed and across the entire farm.

Feed inward history

Date-wise entry for feed inward by type such as pre-starter, starter, and finisher.

Medicine records

Medicine available, medicine required, and medicine administered to birds.

Farm issue reporting

Raise farm-side issues around stock shortage, medicine need, or shed concerns.

Field coordination

Clearer communication between farmer, field officer, and operations team.

Operational Value

A farmer app that improves discipline at the farm level.

For the company, the value is not only convenience. It creates stronger operational records, faster issue reporting, better stock visibility, and a more connected relationship between the farm and the company network.

Faster reporting

Daily records move digitally instead of depending on fragmented calls or registers.

Lower disputes

Clearer visibility into farm-side feed, mortality, and medicine records.

Better control

Company teams can monitor operational issues with stronger consistency.

Scalable model

The workflow becomes easier to manage as the farmer network grows.
